Common Ground

A developing platform designed to help immigrants access reliable legal, healthcare, and community resources in the United States.

Mission

Common Ground aims to build a centralized, searchable database of verified information so families can access support without navigating confusing bureaucratic systems alone.

Who It Helps

The platform is intended for immigrants and their families who need clearer access to trusted information during major transitions involving legal status, healthcare, education, housing, and local support services.

Planned Features

  • Searchable database of verified resources
  • Listings for legal, healthcare, housing, and community support
  • Simple filtering by category and location
  • Clear, accessible design for first-time users

Sample Resource Areas

  • Immigration legal aid
  • Low-cost healthcare clinics
  • Food and housing assistance
  • Language learning and community programs
