Common Ground

Common Ground is a developing platform designed to help immigrants quickly find reliable legal, healthcare, and community resources in the United States.

Mission

The goal is to build a centralized database of verified information so families can access the support they need without navigating confusing bureaucratic systems.

Who It Helps

Common Ground is intended for immigrants and their families who need clearer access to trusted information, especially during major transitions involving legal status, healthcare, education, and local support services.

Planned Features

  • Searchable database of verified resources
  • Listings for legal, healthcare, housing, and community support
  • Simple filtering by location and category
  • Clear, accessible design for first-time users

Sample Resource Areas

  • Immigration legal aid
  • Low-cost healthcare clinics
  • Food and housing assistance
  • Language learning and community programs

Development Timeline

February 2026 – Research into platform structure and resource categories
